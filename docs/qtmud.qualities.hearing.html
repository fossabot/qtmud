qtmud.qualities.hearing module¶

Gives the ability to listen to (hear) things.

New in version 0.0.2-feature/textblob.

Adds hearing to a thing, which gives it the ability to listen to things around it.

class qtmud.qualities.hearing.Hearing[source]¶

Bases: object

Adds hearing to a thing it is applied to.

New in version 0.0.2-feature/textblob.

When applied by the manager, gives a thing the ability to hear. In practice, this means giving the thing the listen method, and, if the thing is Commandable, give sit the listen command.
